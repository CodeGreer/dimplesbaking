About Stellar Baking

I'm Stella, I'm homeschooled, and I love to bake. My motto is 'If you can imagine it, I can create it.' Baking can be very fun, but at other times challenging. I'm a young baker and this is a learning process. I've had challenges with stacking cakes, difficult designs, and transporting the final products. I sometimes get stressed and worry something will go wrong. But it always turns out okay in the end. =D

I also love to act, sing, take pictures, and do crafts. I have so many interests, I can't decide what to do for a living. I might own a food truck, be a photographer, or get into acting. Maybe I'll do all three!
