Cakes and Cupcakes

Cakes and cupcakes are custom made to order. You can be as creative as you like! The sky's the limit with cake flavor, icing, toppings, and decorations. A recent creation was a strawberry pineapple cake with raspberry icing, decorated with fresh raspberries, strawberries, and cocktail umbrellas for a beachy theme.

I pride myself in having the most moist and flavorful cakes around. Also available - vegan options and delivery service. To see some of my favorite cakes, check out my Cakes page. And don't forget to visit my Cupcakes page to see some of my favorites there too.
